---
title: Apple Music
---

- Open the [Apple Music website](https://music.apple.com/).
- Open the Developer Tools window (F12).
- Log in to your account and refresh the page.
- In the Developer Tools window, first locate the `Network` tab, then find the `Filter` icon in the row below and select `Fetch/XHR` as the filter condition.
- Select a request, find the `media-user-token` header in the Request Headers, and copy its value.
- Input the copied value into the `Apple Music media-user-token` setting, then click the accept icon on the right.
